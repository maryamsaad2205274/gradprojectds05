About DentalLandmark

AI-powered orthodontic soft-tissue analysis for dentists.

Smarter facial landmark detection

DentalLandmark is a web application that helps dental professionals automatically detect predefined soft-tissue facial landmarks from frontal and lateral patient images.

Frontal + Side Landmark Overlay Case History PDF Reports

Our Mission

Improve accuracy and reduce manual effort in orthodontic analysis.

  • Faster landmark detection for clinical workflows
  • More consistent analysis compared to manual marking
  • Clear results with editable landmarks when needed
  • Secure patient/case organization and reporting
